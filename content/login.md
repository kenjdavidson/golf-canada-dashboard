# Welcome to Golf Canada Dashboard

This is an unofficial dashboard application that provides a cleaner and more informative interface for Golf Canada services.

## Login Required

Please log in using your Golf Canada credentials to access your golf information, scores, and statistics.

## Important Information

- This application uses your Golf Canada account credentials
- Your data is fetched directly from Golf Canada's services
- We do not store your credentials or personal information
- All data is subject to Golf Canada's terms and policies

For more information about how we handle your information, please review our:

- [Privacy Policy](/privacy)
- [Terms of Service](/terms)

## Disclaimer

This is an unofficial application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Golf Canada in any way.
