# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 24, 2025**

## Overview

Golf Canada Dashboard is an unofficial, open-source web application that provides a user interface wrapper around existing Golf Canada features. This privacy policy outlines how we handle information when you use this application.

## Information Collection and Storage

**We do not capture, collect, or store any personal information.** This application:

- Does not maintain any databases for user information
- Does not store your Golf Canada credentials
- Does not track your usage or browsing behavior
- Does not use cookies for tracking purposes
- Does not share any information with third parties

All authentication and data interactions occur directly between your browser and Golf Canada's services.

## Golf Canada Content

All golf-related content, scores, and information displayed in this dashboard are:

- Provided by Golf Canada
- Subject to Golf Canada's terms of service and policies
- Subject to change without notice based on Golf Canada's updates and regulations
- Governed by Golf Canada's own privacy practices

## Third-Party Services

This application acts as a wrapper around Golf Canada's existing features. When you use this application, you are interacting with Golf Canada's services, which are governed by their own privacy policy and terms of service.

**For information about how Golf Canada handles your data, please refer to their official privacy policy:**  
[https://www.golfcanada.ca/privacy-policy/](https://www.golfcanada.ca/privacy-policy/)

## Open Source Commitment

This project is maintained as open-source software to ensure transparency and build trust with our users. You can review the source code at any time to verify that no information is being collected or stored.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
